How it works

Two Wednesdays per month

We hold two practice evenings per month at All Saints Junior School in Fleet (the same venue as the Monday class). These are run by Jude & Katie.

You are welcome to attend and practise solo, but please note that there will be no partners available on the night.

All session times are 7:00 pm to 8:30 pm. Contact Jude directly for further information on 07413 678040.
