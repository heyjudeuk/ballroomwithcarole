About the classes

Friendly, structured classes for adults at every level

Whether you are starting from scratch or returning to dancing after time away, the classes are designed to be clear, relaxed, and easy to follow. Each session builds step by step, giving you time to understand the dances properly while enjoying the process.

Welcoming and relaxed

A supportive environment where you can learn without pressure and enjoy the experience from the start.

Structured teaching

Lessons are taught in a clear, progressive way so you can build confidence week by week.

No partner required

You can attend with a partner, with a friend, or on your own and still take part fully.

Meet Carole

Experienced, supportive, and passionate about dance

Carole Hellewell is a Ballroom and Latin dance teacher based in Hampshire, with a lifelong background in both social and competitive dancing. Trained from an early age by Peggy Spencer and Len Goodman, she turned professional at 16 and has taught many hundreds of students in social dancing, examinations, and competition work.

She teaches adult Ballroom and Latin classes in Fleet, as well as private lessons and wedding dance tuition.

Her teaching combines experience, warmth, and clarity, helping dancers of all levels feel comfortable while making steady progress.

A few common questions

Everything you need to know to get started

Do I need a partner?

Not always. Some students attend with a partner, while others come with a friend or on their own. Beginners can still learn and practise with guidance in class.

What should I wear?

Just wear something comfortable. If you continue regularly, dance shoes with suede soles can be helpful, but they are not essential for your first visit.

Will I be out of my depth?

No. Classes are grouped by level, and the atmosphere is friendly and supportive. You are encouraged to learn at your own pace.

Do you offer private lessons?

Yes. Private tuition and wedding dance tuition are both available if you would prefer something more personal or tailored to a special occasion.
